# Design Notes

## Desired Feel

Meraki Roots should feel:

- clean
- restrained
- trustworthy
- product-first
- modern without feeling trendy or fragile

## What To Preserve

- the restrained storefront tone
- product imagery as a first-class surface
- a calm palette
- a sense of transparency and care

## What To Avoid

- generic SaaS styling
- purple gradients
- over-designed cards everywhere
- tiny product photography
- cluttered template chrome
- loud novelty motion
- placeholder-feeling stock visuals

## Visual Priorities

1. Product pages should feel intentional.
2. Category imagery should make browsing easier.
3. Homepage should guide visitors, not just decorate.
4. Lab-results and trust surfaces should feel clear and credible.

## Typography

- readable
- confident
- not decorative for its own sake

## Color

- neutral base
- strong contrast where needed
- restrained accent use

## Layout

- let product imagery breathe
- keep navigation predictable
- do not force everything into cards
- mobile layouts must not collapse into crowded stacks

## Trust UX

Trust should be shown through:

- clear labels
- clean ingredient/use/warning sections
- obvious COA access
- consistent compliance language

not through noisy badge spam.
