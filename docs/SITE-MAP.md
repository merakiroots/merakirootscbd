# Site Map

## Core Public Pages

### Homepage

Purpose:

- establish brand trust fast
- guide shoppers to top categories
- make lab transparency feel real
- support wholesale/partner discovery

Must include:

- strong hero
- category access
- trust/transparency signal
- learning/education signal
- partner or wholesale path

### Shop

Purpose:

- clean catalog browsing
- category clarity
- obvious product imagery and price visibility

Must include:

- readable archive title and intro
- product cards that work on mobile and desktop
- usable category browsing

### Product Category Pages

Purpose:

- orient the shopper quickly
- show the product line clearly

Must include:

- correct category title
- correct category image or banner when available
- clean product loop

### Single Product Pages

Purpose:

- convert
- build trust
- reduce hesitation

Must include:

- title
- price
- images
- add to cart
- ingredients
- suggested use
- warnings
- current COA access when available

### Lab Results

Purpose:

- let customers verify current COAs easily
- reinforce transparency

Must include:

- grouped or searchable COA output
- clear PDF access
- no “load whole catalog and filter badly in PHP” anti-pattern

### Partner

Purpose:

- support wholesale/retail inquiries

Must include:

- credible intro
- working contact or form path

### Contact

Purpose:

- provide a straightforward way to reach the business

Must include:

- real contact path
- no obvious placeholder copy

## Global Surfaces

### Header

- desktop nav works
- mobile nav works
- brand is visible
- no layout collisions

### Footer

- key trust/legal links
- contact path
- no junk links

## Launch Rule

If a lower-traffic page is imperfect but the primary shopping and trust flows are strong, prioritize the primary flows first.
